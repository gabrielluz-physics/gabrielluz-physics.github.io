Hi there, I'm Gabriel Luz Almeida!

I'm a Brazilian PhD student in physics working on Gavitational Waves at UFRN - Brazil, and I integrate the gravitational-waves research group of the International Institute of Physics (IIP-UFRN). My interests lie more particularly in understanding how the gravitational radiation emitted by the coalescence of a binary system of compact objects (namely Black Holes or Neutron Stars) can be more efficiently described within the framework of an effective field theory, making use of scattering-amplitude methods.
