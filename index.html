The first GW detection

The first detection of gravitational waves took place on the 14th of September, 2015, by the two LIGO (Laser Interferometer Gravitational-Wave Observatory) detectors. This is the first direct detection of gravitational waves, and first observation of a binary black hole merger, which was then named GW150914. Click here to read more.
